9 Sustainable Shoe Brands For Men (2023)
WRITTEN BY
OUR EDITORS
june 14, 2023
The Good Trade editors endorse products we’ve personally researched, tested, and genuinely love. Learn more about our methodology and business model here .

While many apparel and lifestyle brands have made the switch to fair trade practices, that’s not always the case for footwear production. Often, shoes are manufactured in potentially dangerous working conditions or with forced labor. But it doesn’t have to be this way. Isn’t it time our shoes matched our ethics?

We’ve rounded up a list of sustainable men’s shoes for you! These fair trade shoe brands are making everything from sustainable men’s sneakers to work boots to water shoes while ensuring fair wages, full-scale benefits, and safe working environments for their employees. So whether you need kicks for a night out on the town or laid-back days in the office, these sustainable and ethical footwear companies have you covered.

1. Allbirds

  • Best For

    Athleisure shoes

  • Ethics

    Certified B Corp, natural materials & recycled packaging, WRAP-certified production facility, gives back

  • Size Range

    8–14

  • Price Range

    $50–$170

Imagine the coziest shoes in the world that aren’t just sustainable, but fair trade, too. Welcome to Allbirds . Made with materials like merino wool or eucalyptus tree fibers, Allbirds uses sustainable sourcing methods whenever possible, right down to the recycled plastic shoelaces. As a certified B Corp, the brand also gives back. For errands, walks in the park, or just lounging around, these fair trade shoes belong in every closet.
